About Me

Currently, I am a Mail Carrier with the United States Postal Service. Although this is a good job, I have alot of downtime in the Summer when mail and packages are lite. I am not one to sit around and prefer to stay busy and even learn new things. That's how I came across the Code Kentucky program, and I'm so glad that I did. Not only am I learning a new skill, but I might actually be able to use this skill to make some extra income during my downtime. A flexible schedule where I can work as directed from home sounds like the perfect job to me; especially since I spend most of my days outside fighting the elements -- and animals (like the vicious one pictured above). In addition to my newly acquired coding skills, I am an experienced Administrative Assistant who has done just about everything from importing containers to filing payroll taxes. I am good at working by myself or with a team and look forward to showing you what all I can do.
